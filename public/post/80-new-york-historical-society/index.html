Lowy Framing Application

Frame Search Tool Above: The final tool with search contained within the sidebar Lowy specialises in fine art antique and reproduction frames. They work with top galleries and museums in New York and across the world. Their clients are often very busy, and once they have aquired a work of art, they just want a frame that fits their decor, budget and the work of art itself. This intranet tool allows high-quality capture of frames and virtual compositing of customers’ works of art with Lowy’s frames.
