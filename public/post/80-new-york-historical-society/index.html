New-York Historical Society American Revolution

2003 - 2006 New-York Historical Society American Revolution Digital Media Project The web site made the N-YHS’s extensive revolution-related collection of artifacts and documents available through digitization and dissemenation through the Web. * Web based collections management application (CMA) used by many researchers. * CMA content feeds straight into website collection. * Build-an-exhibition feature (Nexhibition) enables students and visitors to express their views through the collection.
